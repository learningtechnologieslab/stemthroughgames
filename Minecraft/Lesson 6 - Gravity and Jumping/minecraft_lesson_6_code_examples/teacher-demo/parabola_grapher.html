⛏️ Jump Arc — Parabola Demonstration

Teacher Demo · STEM through Games Day 6 · Minecraft Education
JUMP_FORCE (v₀)
Initial velocity 10.0
Positive = upward (Minecraft). In Godot: JUMP_VELOCITY = −500 (negative)
GRAVITY (g)
Per-tick acceleration −0.60
Negative = downward. In Godot: get_gravity() * delta (positive value)
Parabola Formula
y = 10t − 0.3t²
y = position  |  t = tick number
v₀ = JUMP_FORCE  |  g = GRAVITY
t² term makes it a quadratic → parabola!
